THE STORY BEHIND- JD Solitaire
  • JD Solitaire is the brain child of Dr. Sanjay Kalsi, the founder of the company. It is his undying passion and untiring efforts that he has made a name for himself and created the glorious journey of JD Solitaire – The brand that stands for trust, authenticity, fair business and unparalleled customer experience.
  • The journey began over three decades ago:
  • 1992: Dr. Kalsi became an accredited gemologist & started his journey in the jewellery industry. He gained experience while working in multiple business operations including training in jewellery factories & top retailers of the city & selling jewellery to both customers & businesses.
  • 1993: With the experience he gained, Dr. Kalsi made his first small diamond collection, which soon gained a considerable number of customers. With his warm and welcoming nature, he gained the trust of numerous customors.
  • 1996: With a passion to bring the art of jewellery to the masses at affordable prices, Dr. Kalsi started his venture and opened the first jewellery store in Lajpat Nagar, Delhi. He is a first generation enrepreneur.
  • 2006: The company had already garnered a significant reputation and the business expanded to other countries as well. it was the strategic thinking and execution on the part of Dr. Kalsi, which unfolded soveral opportunities and made for a successful business.
  • 2017:  It was a golden year for the brand. Dr. Kalsi expanded his business & opened another shop in Lajpat Nagar, Delhi. Having started with Diamonds, by now JD Solitaire had expanded its competencies to other materials like 22 Karat gold to become a complete jewellery brand in itself. JD Solitaire has come a long way now offering around 2,900 designs. JD Solitaire is well known for their ‘Engagement Rings’ Segment, which is their key USP.
  • 2018 + 2019: JD Solitaire added Silver & Alloy Jewellery in the offering.
  • 2020: Opened second store of Tribe by Amrapali & took a franchise in Mall of India, Noida.
  • 2021: With a passion to improve inventory management, Dr. Kalsi took help from S & S Associates for TOC ( Theory of Constrain ) and has been able to double his inventory turns.
  • 2022: Started his Doctorate Journey at SSBM Geneva Switzerland.
  • Thesis Topic- Impact of AI on Customer Retention: A Customer behavioural perspective in luxury jewellery business in India.
  • Which took him till March of 2024 to complete.
  • 2023: Implemented learnings from Doctorate Degree related to AI in Jewellery Business for which Dr. Sanjay Kalsi, Director got an award for The Retail Jeweller MD and CEO.

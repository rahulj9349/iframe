Our Journey
Welcome to Ornate Jewels: Ornate Jewels Private Limited, where we boast a legacy of 33 years marked by excellence. Our journey began humbly in 1991 with a small shop nestled in Nehru Nagar, spanning a mere 500 sqft and manned by a team of 10 dedicated individuals. Over the years, fueled by a passion for quality and commitment to customer satisfaction, we transitioned to our expansive S.G Road Showroom in 2001, spreading across 2500 sqft and employing approximately 150 skilled professionals. In 2017, we embarked on a new chapter of growth and innovation as we unveiled our newly expanded showroom. Spanning four stories, our state-of-the-art infrastructure now encompasses an impressive 28,000 sqft, serving as a testament to our dedication to providing unparalleled experiences to our valued patrons.
